Color Contrast Tool

This is a tool made to test the percieved brightness of and contrast between different colors.
The WC3 and sRGB weighings, as well as their weightings being inputed into a weighted Euclidean distance (labeled sRGB.G and W3C.G, respectively), are included for your enlightenment.

Suggested Instructions

1. Blue v. White v. Red
2. Red v. White v. Green
3. Green v. White v. Blue
4. Blue v. Green v. Red
5. Yellow v. Cyan v. Magenta
6. Yellow v. White v. Magenta
7. Cyan v. White v. Magenta
8. Cyan v. White v. Yellow
